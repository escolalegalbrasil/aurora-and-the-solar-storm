Aurora and the GREAT SOLAR STORM

CHAPTER 1

Aurora and Peter Meet

My parents met when they were just kids, at a fun and exciting place called the Great World Camp for Young Scientists.

My mother, Aurora, dreamed of becoming an astronaut, exploring the universe, uncovering its mysteries, and traveling among the stars. She loved watching the night sky and all its wonders, but her biggest passion was studying space weather.

“Space weather is like the weather on Earth,” she used to explain to my dad, “except it happens in space and is caused by the Sun. It can create amazing things like auroras, but it can also be dangerous for our satellites, power grids, and communication systems. Scientists study it to understand it better and to protect us.”

My father, Peter, wanted to study agronomy and become a great farmer. He often tried to teach my mother about crops and soil, but she never quite had the talent for that. Still, there was one part she absolutely loved: technology.

“We use technology in moisture sensors and irrigation systems,” my father would say proudly. “It makes farming much more efficient.”

Over time, they fell in love, got married, and…

Suddenly my mother called...

“Bye, Caio! I have to go now!” I said as I waved goodbye to my friend.

“See you later, Stella!” he replied.

When I got home, I found my mom bursting with excitement. She and my dad asked me to sit down. Apparently, they had some big news. I didn’t understand everything, but I caught one word: space.

In that moment, my eyes filled with tears. My mother was going to space, into the vast, endless vacuum of the universe. I felt a wave of sadness, but also incredible joy. Her dream, the one she’d had since she was a little girl, was finally coming true.

CHAPTER 2

Aurora´s Journey to Space

The big day had finally arrived! My mom was about to fulfill her childhood dream and finally see outer space! But not everything went as expected.

When everything was ready, she was already inside the solar research rocket. The launch took place with a huge crowd watching, both in person and online. She felt an overwhelming happiness as the rocket rose into the sky.

For the first few days in space, everything went perfectly. But then the worst happened. A massive solar storm hit Aurora’s spacecraft, damaging the communication system. Without it, she couldn’t contact Earth, and that meant she couldn’t come back because there was no way to signal that it was time to begin the landing preparations.

Aurora was terrified, but deep down she knew there was still hope. If she could repair the ship, she might just find a way to make it home.

CHAPTER 3

The Journalist vs. The Influencer

Meanwhile, here on Earth, everyone was watching.

“We are making history for the first time. We are sending a Brazilian woman to space. But what will she do up there?” you might be wondering. “She will take part in a major mission, studying space weather,” the journalist said excitedly.

When the storm hit, the lights started flickering like in a movie. The scientists panicked. The world stopped. All that echoed in the control room was the phrase, “We’ve lost communication with the spacecraft,” and worse, it was being broadcast for everyone to see.

The media exploded. The influencer then recorded a video titled, “The Worst Has Happened to Aurora.” What do you mean? My mom?

In the video, he said: “Unfortunately, we have an astronaut lost in space. Probably the worst has happened. Well, I hope everyone finds some comfort in this news.”

That had to be a lie. To make things worse, all anyone could see was a viral meme of the journalist.

This started a battle of information between the two of them…

CHAPTER 4

Unwell and Without GPS

The influencer posted yet another fake news video. This time, the title was How Solar Storms affect our wellbeing. Many people watched the video, which said, “From this moment on, one in ten people will feel unwell!” How ridiculous.

The worst part was that many viewers started believing they were actually feeling sick. When they went to the hospital, doctors would tell them, “Sir, you are just very nervous. There are no signs of illness.”

A doctor named Bruna wanted to set the record straight. She treated several patients, and all of them said the same thing, “I saw it in a video on the internet that…” She took notes and asked several journalists to help her spread the word that it was fake news. But nobody wanted to help because they said the influencer would ridicule them, and they didn’t want that.

The only one willing to help was the journalist whom the influencer had made fun of through memes. The journalist and Dr. Bruna recorded a news report designed to grab everyone’s attention.

But when the report first aired on TV, it became a joke. The influencer mocked them, saying they were people trying to “hide the truth” from the public.

Meanwhile, in a place far from all of this, Helena, a driver, and Caio, Helena’s son and Stella’s friend, were struggling with the solar storm. During a trip, their GPS stopped working because of the storm, and Helena and Caio were stranded on a dirt road with forest on both sides. They didn’t know what to do.

Caio decided to check if anything unusual was happening. He discovered that a solar storm was occurring. But when he looked into it further, he saw the influencer’s video and immediately believed it.
